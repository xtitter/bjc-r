Try the following out:
  • Make a sprite keep moving around the screen randomly.
  • Now, make your sprite move around randomly, but still stay within 100 pixels (in the x- and y- directions) of the center of the screen (0, 0). You can do this as follows:
    • If the character is below the y = -100 line, they should not go any lower.
    • If the character is above the y = 100 line, they should not go any higher.
    • If they are to the left of the x = -100 line, they should not go any farther left.
    • If they are to the right of the x = 100 line, they should not go any farther right.
